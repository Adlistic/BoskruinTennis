Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about membership and club activities

How do I become a member?
Use our membership calculator above to determine your fees, then contact us using the form below or email the treasurer at jaichkitty@gmail.com. You'll receive an invoice for payment, and once processed, you'll get access to all member benefits including court keys and booking privileges.
Can visitors play at the club?
Absolutely! Visitors are welcome to use courts after paying the ad-hoc fee (R50/player/hour) at the Clubhouse. You can also join our social tennis sessions. Note that visitors cannot pre-book courts - courts are available on a first-come basis.
What are the benefits of membership?
Members enjoy free court usage, priority court booking, keys to courts and light boxes, league participation eligibility, entry to Club Championships, discounted social tennis fees, and access to member-only events. Plus, you become part of a vibrant tennis community!
Do you offer tennis lessons for beginners?
Yes! Our qualified coaches offer lessons for all ages and skill levels, from mini tennis (age 4+) to advanced squad training. We have group lessons and individual coaching available. Contact Wayne at tennis@waynekets.co.za or WhatsApp 083 703 1434 for more information.
How do I book a court?
Members can book courts online through our SportyHQ platform. Simply visit sportyhq.com, log in with your member credentials, and select your preferred time slot. Bookings can be made up to 7 days in advance. Visitors cannot pre-book but can use available courts on a pay-as-you-go basis.
When are the tennis socials held?
Tuesday evenings from 6pm, Saturday afternoons from 2pm, and Ladies' Group on Tuesday & Thursday mornings at 8am. No booking needed - just arrive at the Clubhouse, register, and we'll match you with players of similar ability. All levels welcome!
